    		Whisk together Egg Mixture in a bowl. Vigorous whisk = avoid cinnamon floating on top. Melt 15g / 1 tbsp butter in a non stick skillet over medium heat. Dunk a piece of bread quickly into the egg mixture, coating both sides. Place in pan. Repeat with more slices to fill the pan, but don’t crowd it (do 2 or 3 at a time). Cook for 2 ½ - 3 minutes on each side until the surface is golden, then transfer to serving plates. Add more butter into the pan and cook remaining bread. Serve with butter and plenty of maple syrup, and Macerated Strawberries if using! - 6 slices brioche or white sandwich bread
    - 2 – 3 tbsp butter
    - 2 large eggs
    - 125 ml milk
    - 1/2 tsp cinnamon powder
    - 1 tsp vanilla extract
